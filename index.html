Welcome to my Calculator

This calculator has incredibly limited functionality, but will eventually be
added to. As it stands, the calculator does single digit addition, subtraction,
multiplication, and division. The Positive/Negative modifier and the percent
modifiers are both disabled, as well as the decimal point. The All Clear button
uses a jQuery call to refresh the page, and all division is rounded off to 6
decimal places. Enjoy!
